XControl

11/23 2016 9:55
Hao Wang
Print

When we test the robot, it's really not a good experience to see the sensor values on the small screen. That's why we are developing XControl, it can read and write the values of robots on the phone and other devices by the HC-06 bluetooth module. Although there's already a lot of bluetooth controllers of MCU, XControl has many unique features, for example, it will allow the users to bind any sensors they use. We decide to develop the android version first and put the app on google play next summer. Please look forward to it if you are interested in.
