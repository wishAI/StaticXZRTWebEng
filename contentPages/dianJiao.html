DianJiao 2013

11/21 2016 11:15
Hao Wang
Print

At the start of summer in 2013, we took pert in the DianJiao Race. This race is totally a different world from RCJ, most of robots in the race are sold by companies and competition are more and more fierce these years, some of these companies even put magnetite and IR transmitter in their robots to interfere the opponent robots. It's harder and harder for us to build our own robots and make some achievements in the race.

We had both basketball and soccer team in the race and made several unique features on our robots. Our basketball striker robot could throw out the ball both side. Also, we used three omniwheels instead of four on the soccer striker robot so it could have a wide groove on each side to get the ball and carry it on the wall.

our basketball striker robot

However, our soccer team never won a race until they were eliminated because of the fast speed and huge power of the robots made by companies, but our way of carrying the ball still gave the opponent a surprise.

our soccer team members and the robots

Our basketball team was a little luckier, we met teams that were not very strong at first, they used the old robots which were bought years ago. However, our striker robot had little chance to catch the ball in the semifinal. Finally, we got the 3rd place in the race.
