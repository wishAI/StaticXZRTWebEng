The New CNC Machine

11/15 2016 14:09
Hao Wang
Print

After almost ten years of usage, our old CNC machine reached its life-span. This machine helped us to make the boards of our robots, all of the robots' components need to to be fixed on these boards. This machine was quite important so we replaced it with a new one.

our new CNC machine made by Sai Wei

The new CNC machine has a more power motor and can help us to cut many boards in a short time. Also, we can use a computer to control the machine instead of copying converted blueprints into a usb device, which is more effective.
