Camera Sensors

11/23 2016 9:55
Hao Wang
Print

Although the rule of RCJ this year doesn't make us use an orange ball instead of infrared ball in formal races, there will be some events which use it. Also, we think that image recognition is the wave of future in Robocup Junior, so it's important for us to research about camera sensors and install them on our robots. The openmv cam is considered by us because it can process most of information on it own and doesn't take too much resources on the MCU.
