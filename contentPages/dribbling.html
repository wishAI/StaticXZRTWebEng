XZ Dribbling Device

11/23 2016 13:25
Hao Wang
Print

The XZ dribbling device is an outstanding component made by the members of team "XZRT". The rotating speed of it is very fast and it never broke in any matches before.

the motor and gears of the dribbling device

At the beginning, we tried to use reducing motors to make the dribbling device, the speed of it was too low and could not catch the ball when the robot moved. Then, we disassembled the reducing motor and made the dribbling devices with fast rotating speed. Also, we found where to buy the component inside of the motor and didn't need to disassemble reducing motors anymore.

the dribbling device on a board of a robot
