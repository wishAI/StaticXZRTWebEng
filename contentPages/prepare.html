Prepare for RCJ 2017

11/14 2016 15:24
Hao Wang
Print

At this time of every year, new key members of XZRT start to design their own robots for the next Robocup Junior Championship. This year, our members also have many new ideas on the design and programs. They even decide to remove the kicker device and instead, using two dribble devices and throw out the ball to make goals. I have to say it's a bold innovation. Also, camera sensors are planned to add on the robots for more accurate action of robots.
