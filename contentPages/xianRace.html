RCJ 2012 China

11/13 2016 12:03
Hao Wang
Print

On March 22nd, our team members went to Xi An in order to take part in the Robocup Junior China 2012. The kind of competition we were in was the "Open League Soccer". In that year, the ultrasound sensor and the kicker uses compressed air instead of electric magnet are very popular.

Our team members were making the robots adapt to the new environment.

We saw many different kinds of robots when we entered the field. Some of them gave us a deep impression, the team Lu Wan Senior, which was also a team from Shanghai, even started to use steel bottle and compressed carbon dioxide for the kicker. Also, the team "EMM-CS" from Macau made their robots so stable and catch attention of all the other teams.

When the race started formatly, we were worried about the performance of our robots, but the fact showed that we made great robots and it's worth to spend so much time on the robots to follow the ball accurately by a fast speed. We met team "EMM-CS" in the final match. Their metal omniwheels allowed the robots to change direction of movement very quickly. Although we did all what we could do, we lost the game only two goals behind.

Our team members were adjusting the compass before a race.

Finally, we took the second place in the race and were able to go to the World Championship. We were happy about getting the honer by our own efforts.
